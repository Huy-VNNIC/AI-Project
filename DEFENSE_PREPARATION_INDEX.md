## 🎯 CÁC ĐIỂM PHẢI NHỚ TUYỆT ĐỐI

### Về kết quả:
- **R² = 0.78** (model accuracy)
- **MAE = 7.8 PM** (average error)
- **MAPE = 16.2%** (percentage error)
- **Improvement = -30.4%** (vs COCOMO II)
- **Dataset = 947 LOC + 24 FP**

### Về phương pháp:
- **Algorithm: Random Forest** (100 trees, depth 15)
- **Outlier: IQR method** (not Z-score, vì không phân phối chuẩn)
- **Transform: log(x+1)** (chuyển non-linear → linear)
- **Schema: 3 separate models** (LOC, FP, UCP)
- **Validation: 5-fold CV** (mean=0.77, std=0.015)

### Về contribution:
- Hybrid COCOMO II + ML
- Multi-schema support
- High explainability (SHAP)
- Confidence quantification
- Production-ready system

### Về limitation:
- FP data insufficient (24 samples)
- UCP data missing (0 samples)
- Not good for >500 KLOC projects
- AI/ML projects: higher error (31% MAPE)

## 🆘 EMERGENCY CONTACTS & RESOURCES

### Nếu cần giải thích thêm:
- **COCOMO II official:** http://csse.usc.edu/csse/research/COCOMOII/cocomo_main.html
- **Random Forest:** Breiman (2001) paper
- **SHAP explainability:** Lundberg & Lee (2017)
- **Software estimation:** Steve McConnell "Software Estimation"

### Nếu cần refresh statistics:
- **R² (R-squared):** Proportion of variance explained (0-1, higher better)
- **MAE (Mean Absolute Error):** Average absolute difference
- **MAPE (Mean Absolute Percentage Error):** Average % error
- **p-value:** Probability result is by chance (<0.05 = significant)
